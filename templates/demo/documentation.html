Introduction

Customer grievance management system makes complaints management very simple as it automates the process which results in minimal human intervention. It plays a very important role for any business firm or company as it enables the company to retain its old customers by solving their problems almost as fast as they appear. The complaints are also classified according to the departments so that the particular complaint ticket could directly go to the concerned executive and not revolve around different departments. The whole system is also inspired by machine learning for automation, which means that if a solution to a particular problem already exists in the database then automatic reply will be sent for that complaint. The system is also designed to entertain complaints from various channels like email web forms and social media. It’s very flexible and easy to use. A well-equipped dashboard is provided, All the registered customer care executives can simply login to the dashboard where they can find the customer complaints related to their department. They can either send the solution directly or escalate the complaint to the higher authority. The dashboard also shows the profile of the customer which helps the executives to understand the customer complaints better. The system is also well equipped with different analytics options like customer satisfaction survey, executive performance, ticket lifecycle etc. These data will help a company to be updated about the number of happy customers or the performance of the executives and the way the executives are serving the customers. These data are very important for a company to grow and hence are included in the product.

Information Provider

    There are times when a company needs to provide a certain information to all its customers at the same time. This information may be related to a certain sale or discount offer / exchange offer or anything else. With the help of information provider, certain information can be passed to many people at the same time.

Hierarchy structure

    There are two types of login in the system.
    Admin Login
    Admin will get full access to the product. This means an admin can access whole of the dashboard contents including all the charts, reports, Information provider and the profiles of the customers and the executives.
    Executive Login
    This is for the executives who solves the customers’ problems. Executives will get access to the ticket inbox section so that they can look into the incoming complaint tickets and send them the solution message. Also they can view the profile of the customers.
